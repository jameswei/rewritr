Rewritr

Rewrite selected English into smoother, more natural, native-like wording without making it academic, overly formal, slangy, or filler-heavy.

Download beta Learn more

Why it exists

English that sounds closer to what you meant.

Rewritr is built for non-native English speakers who already know what they want to say, but want the final sentence to read more naturally.

Select text in any editable macOS app, trigger Rewritr, and let your configured OpenAI-compatible provider rewrite it. Preview the result first, or replace it instantly when you want the fastest flow.

Capabilities

Focused on a narrow, useful v1.

Natural English rewriting

Preserves meaning while making selected English smoother and more native-like.

Preview or instant replace

Choose a confirmation popup or a faster in-place replacement workflow.

Provider you control

Use your own OpenAI-compatible Chat Completions endpoint, model, and API key.

No account or backend

The app runs locally and sends selected text only to the provider you configure.

macOS-native storage

API keys live in Keychain. Non-sensitive settings live in UserDefaults.

Clear compatibility limits

Works best in apps with normal copy and paste; secure fields and terminals are limited.
